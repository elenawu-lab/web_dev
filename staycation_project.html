Lagoon

Lagoon is unique because of its ride called cannibal. You are raised 208 feet in the air and then dropped 116 degrees beyond vertical free-fall. It also includes many different thrilling rides like Colossus, Roller Coaster, and Wicked. They also have many different kid friendly rides like Odysea, Puff the Little Fire Dragon, and Dinosaur Drop.
