Lagoon

Lagoon is unique because of its ride called cannibal. You are raised 208 feet in the air and then dropped 116 degrees beyond vertical free-fall. It also includes many different thrilling rides like Colossus, Roller Coaster, and Wicked. They also have many different kid friendly rides like Odysea, Puff the Little Fire Dragon, and Dinosaur Drop.

Great Places To Eat At While You Are There

  • Teriyaki Stix
  • Corn Dog Corner
  • Big John's Shake Shack
  • Honolulu Hot Dog
  • Lagoon's Burger Express
  • Carousel Real Potato

Lagoon A Beach

While Lagoon includes so many fun roller coasters, it also has a waterpark! It is called Lagoon A Beach, and it is three whole acres of fun. You can relax floating through the lazy river, viewing the beautiful waterfalls, exotic tunnels, and tropical lagoons. For a thrill, you will want to go to the jet slides, where you can slide your heart out.
