Lobsters

What They Are

Lobsters are a marine animal. There are 16 different types, all with varying characteristics. They have a hard shell and large claws. These claws can be very strong and can regenerate if lost. Their diet consists of fish, mollusks, and seafood. Humans are their main predators, they consider lobsters a delicacy and consume and hunt them at high rates. They lay eggs and are closely related to crabs.
